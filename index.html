Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why should we donate blood?

Because blood cannot be manufactured artificially and it saves lives during surgeries, accidents, and medical emergencies.

2. How often can we donate blood?

Every 3 months for men and every 4 months for women, depending on health condition.

3. Is blood donation safe?

Yes, it is completely safe. Sterile equipment is always used and the donor’s health is checked before donation.
